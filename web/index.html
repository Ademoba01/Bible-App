Rhema Study Bible

The Bible that listens and speaks your language.

Rhema Study Bible is a free, open-source Bible companion app available on iOS, Android, and Web. No subscriptions, no paywalls — every feature is free for everyone.

Features

  • AI-Powered Bible Search — Ask natural language questions like "What does the Bible say about fear?" and get relevant Scripture results instantly. No need to memorize exact verses or keywords.
  • Audio Bible — Listen to Scripture with full audio narration. Perfect for commutes, workouts, or daily devotional time. Hands-free Bible listening at no cost.
  • Interactive Bible Maps — Explore the lands of the Bible with interactive maps. See where biblical events took place and trace the journeys of Paul, Moses, and Jesus.
  • Bible Quizzes — Test and grow your Scripture knowledge with interactive quizzes covering Old Testament, New Testament, characters, events, and theology.
  • Multiple Translations — Read and compare Scripture across multiple Bible translations to deepen your understanding of God's word.
  • Daily Devotionals — Start each day with curated Scripture and reflection to guide your spiritual journey.
  • Personalized Study Plans — Follow structured reading plans tailored to your interests and pace.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Rhema Study Bible really free?

Yes, completely free. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads. Every feature is available to all users at no cost. The app is open source.

What makes the AI search different from regular Bible search?

Traditional Bible search requires exact words or references. Rhema's AI search understands natural language — ask "verses about dealing with anxiety" or "what did Jesus say about loving enemies" and get relevant results, even if your exact words don't appear in the text.

Can I listen to the Bible offline?

Rhema Study Bible includes audio narration for hands-free listening. Download chapters for offline access during commutes, workouts, or devotional time.

What Bible translations are available?

Rhema supports multiple Bible translations so you can read Scripture in the version that resonates with you. Compare translations side by side to deepen your understanding.

Is Rhema Study Bible available on iPhone and Android?

Yes, Rhema Study Bible is available on iOS (iPhone and iPad), Android, and as a web app at rhemabibles.com. Use it on any device with a browser.

Is Rhema Study Bible safe for kids?

Yes, Rhema Study Bible is completely safe for children. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, no social features, and no inappropriate content. Kids can explore Scripture through audio narration, quizzes, and maps in a safe, free environment.

Can kids use Rhema Study Bible for Bible study?

Absolutely. Kids can listen to Bible stories with audio, take fun quizzes to test their knowledge, explore interactive maps, and ask questions in their own words using the AI search. It's designed to be accessible for readers of all ages, from children to adults.

Is this a good Bible app for family devotionals?

Yes. Families use Rhema Study Bible for daily devotionals, bedtime Bible stories (with audio), and interactive Bible learning. Parents and children can explore Scripture together using quizzes and maps, making family Bible time engaging and educational.
