Rhema Study Bible

We're sorry to see you go. You can request complete deletion of your Rhema Study Bible account and all associated data.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (email and display name)
  • Community posts and contributions
  • Bookmarks and reading history stored in the cloud
  • All profile data

What is NOT affected

  • Data stored only on your device (local preferences, font size, etc.) — clear this by uninstalling the app
Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use cloud features again.
Delete from the app: You can also delete your account directly in the app by going to Settings → Profile → Delete Account.
