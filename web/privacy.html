Privacy Policy

Rhema Study Bible

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the Rhema Study Bible app ("App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name through Firebase Authentication. This is optional — the app works fully without an account.

Reading Preferences: Your chosen translation, font size, dark mode setting, and bookmarks are stored locally on your device using shared preferences.

Usage Data: We do not collect analytics, tracking data, or usage statistics.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your location data
  • We do not collect your contacts or phone number
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels
  • We do not sell or share any personal data with third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Account data is used solely for authentication and syncing your profile across devices
  • Preferences are stored locally to personalize your reading experience
  • AI features (when enabled) send verse text to Google Gemini API for study insights — no personal data is included in these requests

4. Data Storage & Security

Account data is stored securely using Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption. Local preferences remain on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — for optional user accounts (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • Cloud Firestore — for optional community features (Firebase Privacy Policy)
  • Google Gemini API — for optional AI study features, only when user provides their own API key (Google AI Terms)

6. Children's Privacy

The App includes a Kids Mode designed for young readers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Kids Mode operates entirely with locally stored content and does not require an account.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen within the App. Local preferences can be cleared by uninstalling the App.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
